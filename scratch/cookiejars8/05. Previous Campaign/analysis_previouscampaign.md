FINAL FINDINGS AND ANALYSIS
1. How does the outcome of a previous campaign affect current success?

Previous campaign outcome strongly affects current success. Customers with prior success have the highest subscription rates, while those with prior failure have the lowest.

2. Are customers with previous successful campaigns more likely to subscribe again?

Yes. Customers who previously subscribed are significantly more likely to subscribe again in the current campaign.

3. Are customers previously unsuccessful unlikely to convert again?

Yes. Customers who previously did not subscribe tend to have lower conversion rates in future campaigns.

4. Does time since last contact (pdays) influence success probability?

Yes. Customers contacted more recently generally show higher subscription rates compared to those contacted long ago or never contacted.

5. Are existing customers more likely to subscribe than new ones?

Yes. Customers who have been contacted before, especially successfully, are more likely to subscribe than new customers.
